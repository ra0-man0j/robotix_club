Achievements

Our Achievements

Without continual growth and progress, such words as improvement, achievement, and success have no meaning. Happiness does not come from doing easy work but from the afterglow of satisfaction that comes after the achievement of a difficult task that demanded our best.

Our Fellow Roboticists have represented the club at various levels of Robotics Competirions and secured fine positions for the Club.

  • Shell Eco Marathon

    Shell Eco-Marathon challenges students to build ultra-energy efficient vehicles. Participants build automotive vehicles to achieve the highest possible fuel efficiency. The competition is huge and therefore, the Shell Eco Marathon is divided into three zones:Asia, Europe and America.
    We participated in Shell Eco Marathon in 2018, which was held in Singapore.
    While a normal vehicle gets an average of around 60 km per liter of fuel, our innovative design had an average of 1200 km’s from one liter of Petrol.
    We secured 3rd Position in India
    And 17th Position in Asia.

  • ROBOCON

    Robocon (short for Robotic Contest) is organized by Asia-Pacific Broadcasting Union (ABU) A Collection of over 20 countries of Asia Pacific Region.
    In 2018, the competition was held in Vietnam where each country presents a team. Therefore, A Competition to select the
    best team from India was held in march 2018 at MIT Pune.
    A team of 40 members from all years participated in this National Level Competition.

  • Wissenaire'19

    Wissenaire is organised in IIT BHUBANESWAR it's the one of the biggest techno-management fest across the country.
    We Acquired the First Prize in BOX MAZE’19 event where we made a bot which moved along walls avoiding to hit it.
    The group consisted of experienced and hardworking members Consisting of Sri Sai Suhas K, Ayushi Chaudhary, Swapnil Sahu, Mahendra Gabel and Mayank dixit.
    Maze Solver - Best Mechanical Design
    Trekkon , Kick off - Best Electrical Design

  • Sensorous

    We acquired the 3rd Position in sensuous event wherein we had to make a “Semi-Autonomous Bot” that will follow a track of curves, degrees and crossovers.
    The bot had to traverse through sharp turns and unexpected changes. In all this the experienced team of ROBOTiX CLUB performed flawlessly and secured 3rd in the event.

  • Wissenaire'16

    In the Trekkon event of Wissenaire, the team won the First Prize.
    They made a bot which could traverse in a rugged terrain and could pick and put desired objects in right place.
    The team was guided by the able guidance of Ankit Pawar.
    The team performed effortlessly during the event making this one of the most happening events in Wissenaire.

  • Technex’16

    We acquired 2nd Position in the event. The event was organized by IIT BHU wherein we made a bot to traverse though a grid picking objects at different places.
    The competition was fierce as the bot has to compete against international level Robots with teams coming from various countries.

  • Cognizance’15

    We gained 1st Position in the event AHEC.
    This was organised by IIT Roorkee wherein we made a bot to gain maximum projectile range using water bottle jet.
    The bot had to face real life problems to make the most out of the limited resources.

  • Cognizance’15

    In the Posiedon event Of IIT Roorkee, the Team won the First Prize.
    Where the bot had to compete in water maze arena using a fast swimmer bot avoiding all the obstacles.
    The Team was lead by Ankit Pawar. He guided the fellow teammates towards victory.

  • Spazor

    We acquired Third Prize in Spazor.
    Where they had to make an ‘Autonomous Line Follower Bot’ which follows a path made of white lines. The path was haphazard containing 90° turns, bends etc.
    The Team was guided and leaded by the visionary Pankaj Yadav.
